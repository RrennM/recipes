Ingredients

1 row of Ritz crackers
1:2 sugar:water syrup
butter
cinnamon
lemon zest
pie crust and shell

Coarsely break the Ritz crackers in the pie crust. Add lemon zest to 
simple syrup and bring to boil. Let cool and add to Ritz. Add butter 
blots and sprinkle with cinnamon. Add Shell. Bake till golden brown. 
Let cool. Serve with ice cream.
